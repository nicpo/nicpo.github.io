Hey - I'm a **data scientist and AI researcher**. I work across the full stack of AI and production ML: building evaluation infrastructure for AI tools at scale, deploying inference pipelines conforming to FDA regulations and NLP for legal and enterprise products. I've led AI teams, shipped to production, and measured the revenue impact of what I build. Two US patents and an ACL publication came out of that work.

I also spent 13 years as an **Associate Professor** of international business at Copenhagen Business School, where I researched social media dynamics and technology strategy. My Coursera course on [strategy implementation](https://www.coursera.org/learn/strategy-implementation) has over 35,000 learners.

In 2025 I published [**Mass Automation: Rethinking Companies for an AI Era When They Can Operate Autonomously**](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0FV2CMBF8/). It blends my background as an engineer and business school professor and discusses organizations when AI can run core business functions. There's a public companion tracker at [massautomation.io](https://massautomation.io).
